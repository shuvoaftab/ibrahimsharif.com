Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Content Delivery Network (CDN)?

A CDN, provided by Me, is a network of strategically placed servers designed to optimize the delivery of digital content to end-users. It caches and distributes your website's content, reducing load times and enhancing the user experience, regardless of their geographic location.

Why do I need a CDN for my website?

I recommend using a CDN to speed up your website, improve its performance, and enhance security. By serving content from servers closer to your visitors, a CDN reduces latency, handles traffic spikes, and mitigates DDoS attacks, resulting in a better user experience and higher SEO rankings.

What security measures are included in your CDN services?

I offer DDoS Protection and Mitigation, Secure CDN Solutions, and encryption to safeguard your content. These measures protect against cyber threats, unauthorized access, and data breaches, ensuring the security and integrity of your digital assets.

Can I customize CDN policies to suit my specific needs?

Yes, you have the flexibility to set custom Content Rules and Policies with My services. This allows you to tailor the CDN to your unique requirements, ensuring that content is delivered and managed exactly as you desire.
