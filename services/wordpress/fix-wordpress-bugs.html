Frequently Asked Questions

What is WordPress debugging, and why do I need it?

WordPress debugging is the process of identifying and fixing issues or bugs within your WordPress website's code and functionality. It's essential to maintain a smooth and error-free website experience for your visitors.

How do I know if my WordPress site has bugs or issues?

Common signs include broken links, slow loading times, error messages, layout problems, or unexpected behavior. If you're unsure, our service includes a comprehensive site audit to pinpoint issues.

How long does it take to fix WordPress bugs?

The time required depends on the complexity and number of issues. Simple problems might be resolved within hours, while more intricate ones could take a few days. We'll provide an estimate after reviewing your site.

Is bug fixing included in ongoing website maintenance?

Bug fixing may be included in maintenance packages. We offer both one-time bug fixing and ongoing maintenance services tailored to your needs.
