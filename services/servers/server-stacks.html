Frequently Asked Questions

What is a server stack, and why do I need it for my website?

A server stack is a combination of software components like NGINX, Apache, MySQL, and PHP, crucial for hosting websites and applications. It ensures your site runs smoothly and securely.

How often will my server stack be updated and optimized?

Regular updates and optimizations to keep your stack secure and performing at its best.

What are Common Server Performance Issues?

Common issues include slow page loading, unresponsive servers during traffic spikes, high server resource utilization, and security vulnerabilities. These issues can hinder the performance of your website or application.

Can I request custom configurations or additional software installations?

Yes, I can accommodate custom configurations and additional software installations based on your requirements.
