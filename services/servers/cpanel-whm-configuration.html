Overview of cPanel/WHM Configuration Service

My cPanel/WHM configuration service offers comprehensive solutions for web hosting optimization. From seamless website migration to robust email management and enhanced security measures, I tailor our services to elevate your online presence. With a focus on performance optimization and data security, I ensure that your website operates efficiently and securely, providing a seamless experience for both you and your users.

Why this service?

Don't miss out on the opportunity to make your web hosting experience exceptional!

Features of my cPanel/WHM Service:

  • Install the cPanel/WHM software on a fresh server.
  • Configure the server's hostname and DNS settings.
  • Configure the server's primary network settings, including hostname, domain, and resolver configuration.
  • Create individual cPanel accounts for hosting customers.
  • Enable SSL/TLS for secure connections.
  • Configure essential services such as Apache, PHP, MySQL, and Exim (email server) to optimize performance and security.

Choosing my cPanel/WHM service means unlocking a world of effortless website management, enhanced security, and top-notch support. With me, your online presence will not only thrive but also stay safe and secure.
